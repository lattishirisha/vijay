Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj

Shivaji Maharaj is the founder of Maratha dynasty and a warrior king of Maharashtra.
He is known as Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, He had 8 wives in total.
Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj was born in the Shivneri Fort in Maharashtra on 19 February 1630.
At 15 years, he conquered the Torna Fort.
